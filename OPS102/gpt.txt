ChatGPT is an artificial intelligence chatbot developed by OpenAI and released in November 2022.  

The name "ChatGPT" combines "Chat", referring to its chatbot functionality, and "GPT", which stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer, a type of large language model.  

Wikipedia 

ChatGPT has been trained on huge amount of data scraped from internet. 

This has enabled us to develop artificial programs that can answer questions like humans.
