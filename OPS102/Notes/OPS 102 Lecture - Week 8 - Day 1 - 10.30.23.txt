What is an absolute path?

my answer:
	Path from /root to destination

actual answer:
	Path from the filesystem root to the destination

Which of the following filenames identifies as a hidden file in Linux?

my answer:
	~/bashrc.hid

actual answer:
	~/.bashrc (the . makes it hidden)

What is the primary purpose of 'pipe' in CLI?

my answer:
	To create another process

actual answer:
	To pass the output of one command as input to another

You currently have multiple nano processes running. Terminate all of them without finding their PID

answer:
	pkill -nano

actual answer:
	pkill nano

In Linux, what is the superuser account called?
